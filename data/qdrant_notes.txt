Qdrant is a vector database for storing and searching embeddings.

Useful endpoints:
- GET /readyz : liveness/readiness check

In LangChain, a Qdrant-backed vectorstore can:
- add_texts(texts, metadatas, ids)
- similarity_search(query, k)

Common pitfalls:
- collection name mismatch
- vectors size (embedding dimension) mismatch
- forgetting to ingest/index before querying
